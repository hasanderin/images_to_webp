WEBP Image Optimizer

WebP Image Optimizer is a powerful module designed to enhance the performance and loading speed of your Odoo website by optimizing the images on your website in the WebP format without compromising the quality, as well as further adjusting the image performance.

WebP, developed by Google, is a modern image format that offers superior compression compared to other image formats, all while preserving the quality of your visuals.

With WebP Image Optimizer, you can adjust various image formats on your website with a single click, significantly reducing the file size of your images and speeding up the website loading time.

What is the WebP format?

WebP is an image format developed by Google in 2010 that aims to provide high-quality image compression and smaller file sizes compared to more well-known image formats like JPEG and PNG.

What are the advantages of the WebP format?

Some of the main benefits of the WebP format are the support of lossy and lossless compression modes, smaller file sizes, support of transparent image backgrounds, browser support, and quality and compression efficiency.

Can I set up my desired image resolution with the WebP image optimizer module?

Yes, with the WebP module, you can choose how much you want your pictures to be compressed, to achieve the resolution you are looking for.

Will the quality of my images suffer?

One of the key advantages of the WebP format is that it compresses the images significantly without affecting the quality of the images or affecting them minimally.
